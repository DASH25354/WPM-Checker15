Three-minute Training

【15】Homework is my biggest stress. If I had a clear plan, I’d start right after school and be done by dinner. I wish math were easier; sometimes I just stare at one problem for thirty minutes. I act as if I were calm in class, but my hands shake before I check my answers. If I had someone to ask, I’d learn faster. I’m also worried about the school festival. I wish our team were kinder. We act as if we were a perfect group, but we argue about the poster and who’s in charge. If the teacher were there, we’d listen. I wish we could say sorry and work as one team. If we had a fresh start, we’d make something we were proud of.

Quiz

The student feels stressed by homework (especially math) and wishes to finish efficiently with a clear plan. The student wakes up early every morning to study English and improve grades. The student thinks the school library is quiet and comfortable. The student experiences conflict within the school festival team over the poster and wishes to apologize and work together.
